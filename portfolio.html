A Visual Look at Our Favorite Movies
There are movies we think are good and movies we all pay to go see. These movies are not always the same. This is a visual look at our favorite movies, using IMDb ratings and data of box office ticket sales (adjusted for inflation) from Box Office Mojo.
This project included original data analysis using Excel and findings presented with Google Fusion Tables and Highcharts.
Germany's Run to World Cup Victory
The German men's national soccer team did not lose a single game on the road to winning the 2014 World Cup, their fourth title. This story — told through graphics — documents their path to victory.
This project included original data analysis using Excel and findings presented with Google Fusion Tables and Highcharts.
